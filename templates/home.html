  • Families

    A family unit is the first item you need to add. When initially adding the Family, you can also add Parents or Children, but they are both optional. The family contains the mailing address and home phone number for all the parents and children in the family unit. You can add as many Children and Parents to the Family as you like.


    Go

  • Parents

    The Parent has 3 required fields of First Name, Last Name, and Guardian Type (in the forms, if a Field is in Bold, that means it is required. The Parent also has a Work Phone, Cell Phone, and Email field.


    Go

  • Children

    Children also have a First and Last Name, Gender, and Birth Day (a date field). Their Age is calculated automatically. When viewing a Child form, you can also see any Siblings that they may have in the Siblings tab, and their Family Address information. You make Contacts and Appointments off of the Child form by going to the Contacts and Appointments tab for a specific Child.


    Go

  • Contacts

    The below button (and NavBar button at the top) for Contacts will show all Contacts made. If you go to the Family form, the Contacts you see there will be Contacts made to that family. If you go to a Child's Form, you will see the Contacts made to just that Child. Contacts are added/made by going to the Child form.


    Go

  • Appointments

    Appointments are very similar to Contacts; see above!


    Go

  • Sessions

    Sessions are a completed study with a single child. From sessions, you can also search for children who have not participated in a given study.


    Go

    Studies are currently added through the Admin interface (final NavBar button).
